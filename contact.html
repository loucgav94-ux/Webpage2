🏫 College Plans

After high school, I plan to attend the University of Wisconsin–Madison to major in Mechanical Engineering. UW–Madison is one of the top engineering schools in the country and I can't wait to be part of Badger Nation. The campus, the culture, and the academics all make it the perfect fit.

💼 Career Goals

Long term, I'm interested in working in technology or engineering-related fields — specifically in tech sales or product development. I want a career where I can combine my mechanical engineering background with people skills and strategy to build or sell products that actually solve real problems.

💡 Entrepreneurship

I've already started my own business — MG Window Washing — which has given me a head start on understanding what it takes to run something independently. Whether it's a startup or a large company, I see entrepreneurship playing a big role in my future no matter what path I take.
