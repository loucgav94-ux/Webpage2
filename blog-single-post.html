MY FAVORITE MOVIES

I'm a big fan of movies that are funny, action-packed, or just genuinely entertaining. Cocaine Bear is one of my favorites — it's ridiculous in the best way possible and never gets old. Dodgeball: A True Underdog Story is a classic comedy that I could rewatch a hundred times.

Dirty Grandpa is another go-to — it's hilarious and endlessly quotable. On the more serious side, The Hunger Games is a great action franchise that actually has a solid story behind it. And nothing beats Christmas Vacation during the holidays — it's a tradition at this point.

Overall I tend to gravitate toward movies that don't take themselves too seriously. Whether it's a comedy, an action flick, or a holiday classic, I just want to have a good time watching it.
