the day i was born — august 5

★ ZODIAC SIGN — LEO THE LION

Born on August 5, my zodiac sign is Leo (July 23 – August 22). Leos are known for being confident, competitive, and natural leaders — which fits pretty well. August 5 Leos in particular are described as witty, intelligent, entrepreneurial, and driven to win. Fire is Leo's paired element, meaning passion and determination come naturally. Lucky numbers: 2, 5, 10, 11, 25.

★ FAMOUS PEOPLE BORN ON AUGUST 5

I share a birthday with Neil Armstrong, the first person to walk on the Moon and one of the greatest engineers and explorers in history. Other August 5 birthdays include Patrick Ewing (NBA Hall of Famer), Adam Yauch of the Beastie Boys, and actress Olivia Holt.

★ HISTORICAL EVENTS ON AUGUST 5

August 5 has seen some major moments in history. In 1305, Scottish hero William Wallace was captured by English forces — the same Wallace immortalized in the film Braveheart. In 1905, President Theodore Roosevelt helped broker peace between Russia and Japan, leading to the Treaty of Portsmouth. In 1812, Native American leader Tecumseh led a famous ambush during the War of 1812. It's a date with a lot of history behind it.
